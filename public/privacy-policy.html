Privacy Policy for Yskandar Backup Service

Last updated: September 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our Dropbox backup integration collects and processes the following information:

  • Database Content: We create encrypted backups of your blog database, including posts, comments, user data, and categories.
  • Dropbox Account Access: We access your Dropbox account solely to store backup files in the designated backup folder.
  • Technical Data: Backup timestamps, file sizes, and system logs for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Creating and storing database backups in your personal Dropbox account
  • Managing backup retention (automatically removing old backups)
  • Monitoring backup system health and performance
  • Providing backup restoration capabilities when needed

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Location: All backup files are stored in your personal Dropbox account under the "/yskandar_backups/" folder
  • Encryption: Backup files are compressed and transmitted securely via HTTPS
  • Access: Only you and authorized administrators of the Yskandar blog system can access the backups
  • Retention: We automatically maintain the 4 most recent backups, older backups are automatically deleted

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Your backup data remains private and is only accessible to:

  • You (as the Dropbox account owner)
  • Authorized administrators of the Yskandar blog system

5. Dropbox Integration

Our service integrates with Dropbox to provide backup storage. By using this service, you acknowledge that:

  • Dropbox's own privacy policy applies to data stored in your Dropbox account
  • We only access the specific backup folder created for this purpose
  • You can revoke access to your Dropbox account at any time through your Dropbox settings

6. Data Retention

  • Active Backups: We maintain the 4 most recent backup files in your Dropbox
  • System Logs: Operational logs are retained for 30 days for troubleshooting purposes
  • Account Deletion: If you disable the backup service, existing backup files remain in your Dropbox account but no new backups will be created

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your backup data at any time through your Dropbox account
  • Download or delete any backup files from your Dropbox
  • Revoke Dropbox access permissions through your Dropbox app settings
  • Request information about backup operations affecting your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Yskandar blog contact form or email.

10. Compliance

This backup service is designed for personal use and complies with standard data protection practices. The service processes data solely for backup and data recovery purposes.

This Privacy Policy is specific to the Yskandar Backup Service's Dropbox integration functionality.
